About: Snow Before You Go

Why this site was built:

Any "real" skier has one objective, more powder days. Snow Before You Go is built to achieve one goal - Empower skiers to get more powder days. With the advent of the Ikon pass (RIP Vail), there are so many mountains to choose from across 4 continents and 5 countries, many of which are some of the best ski mountains on the planet. With so many options to choose from, it can be hard to keep track of which mountains are getting the best snow, and how to make sure your next day on the hill is a powder day. See you on the hill, and keep shredding!
